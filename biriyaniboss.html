Our Journey

More Than Just Biryani

BiriyaniBAoss started as a small home kitchen experiment in Amsterdam, driven by a craving for authentic Hyderabadi biryani we couldn't find in the Netherlands. What began as cooking for friends quickly became a beloved community fixture.

Every grain of rice, every whole spice, and every slow-cooked pot is a tribute to generations of culinary tradition. We source premium basmati rice from India, whole spices from specialty importers, and fresh local produce from Dutch markets.

Today we serve thousands of customers across Amsterdam, Rotterdam, and Den Haag — delivering soul food that bridges cultures and brings people together.

Our History

How We Grew

2019

The First Pot

Rajan cooks his mother's Hyderabadi dum biryani for 20 friends in Amsterdam. They demand seconds — and thirds.

2020

Going Online

Launched our first ordering channel via WhatsApp. 50 orders in the first week. A brand is born.

2021

First Commercial Kitchen

Moved into a professional kitchen in Amsterdam-Oost and expanded to Rotterdam deliveries.

2022

Sunday Grand Special Debut

Launched our legendary Sunday-only menu with the Royal Dum Biryani. Sold out every week since.

2023

Party Catering Launch

Introduced Bronze–Platinum packages. Catered 80+ events including weddings and corporate dinners.

2025

Den Haag Expansion

Now serving all three major Dutch cities. 500+ happy customers and growing every week.
